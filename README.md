<section id="about">
    <h1>About</h1>
<pre class="info">
<b>Red Ribbon</b> is a well-known bakery chain from the Philippines, famous for its cakes, pastries, and comfort food.
It's especially popular for celebrations like birthdays and holidays.


<b>Background</b>
Founded in 1979 in Quezon City
Started as a small cake shop and grew into a nationwide (and international) brand
Now owned by Jollibee Foods Corporation


<b>What They're Known For</b>
Red Ribbon is best known for its delicious and affordable desserts:
Cakes
Black Forest Cake
Mango Supreme Cake
Chocolate Mousse Cake
Pastries & Treats
Ensaymada
Ube Cake
Butter Mamon


<b>Locations</b>
Hundreds of stores across the Philippines
International branches in countries like the U.S., Canada, and parts of the Middle East
